Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients And Quantity
plain bread 2 slices
grape jelly 3 tbsp
peanut butter 3.5 tbsp
cinnamon powder 2 tsp

Step 1: Lightly toast the bread.

Step 2: Smear the peanut butter over one slice of the bread.

Step 3: Smear the jelly over the other slice of bread.

Step 4: Sprinkle a conservative amount of cinnamon powder over either slice.

Step 5: Put the slices of bread together and cut into 2 triangles.
